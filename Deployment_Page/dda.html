Deckr Desktop Flickr Application

No longer do you have to waste time opening your browser to upload images to or viewing photos from Flickr.

What is Deckr?

Deckr is a Desktop AIR application that allows you to quickly view from or upload photos to Flickr. No longer do you have to open your browser to share your photos. Upload them instantly right from the convienence of your desktop.

What to look for

Deckr brings proffessional photographers as well as photo enthusiest ease and convienence when it comes to sharing your best pictures. Providing you with a simplier way of uploading your photographs to Flickr without having to go to your browser. You can do the same things within the Deckr application as you could on the Flickr site and that is upload your photos, title and describe your photographs, and share them on the Flickr site.

Not only are you able to share your photos that you take with everyone on Flickr, you will also be able to view the photos of your friends and family right from your desktop. But if you decide that you want to go to the site, Deckr also provides a way to log into the Flickr site.

Future implementations

Deckr is currently in its first version, but will be expanding soon. Deckr will bring to life in future versions the ability to not only share your photos within Flickr but also other social sites such as Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, and the list goes on. No matter where you want to share your photos your Deckr application will be there to provide you a simplier way to put your photos in front of the people that want to see them the most.
